Main Dishes & Savory
Christmas Morning Wife Saver
* Ingredients:
   * 16 slices of bread (crusts removed)
   * Thinly sliced bacon or ham
   * Slices of sharp cheddar cheese
   * 6 eggs
   * 1/2 tsp salt
   * 1/4 tsp pepper
   * 1/2 - 1 tsp dry mustard
   * 1/4 cup minced onion
   * 1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
   * 1-2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
   * Dash Tabasco or red pepper sauce
   * 3 cups milk
   * 1/2 cup butter
   * Crushed Cornflakes
* Instructions: Arrange 8 slices of bread in the bottom of a buttered 9x13 pan. Cover bread with a layer of ham, then cheese. Cover with remaining bread slices (like a sandwich). Beat eggs with salt & pepper. Add dry mustard, onion, green pepper, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco sauce & milk. Pour over sandwiches. Cover & refrigerate overnight. In the morning, melt butter, pour over top & sprinkle with crushed cornflakes. Bake uncovered 1 hour at 350°F.

Chicken Dip (Mexican Layered Dip)
* Ingredients:
   * 2 cups cooked, chopped chicken (boneless, skinless works best)
   * 1 cup onion
   * 2 tbsp oil
   * 1/4 cup jalapeno peppers
   * 1 cup salsa
   * 1 can cream of mushroom soup
   * 1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese (grated)
   * 1 1/2 cup mozza cheese (grated)
* Instructions: Sauté onion in oil, add peppers and mix well to heated cream soup mixture. Add most of the cheese (save small amount to put on top) and melt slowly to a thick, creamy mixture. Line a casserole dish with tortilla wraps and then layer salsa, chopped chicken, and creamy cheese mixture and repeat. Top with remaining cheese and bake for 1/2 hour or less until hot through.
Russian Chicken
* Ingredients:
   * Chicken breast
   * 1/2 bottle Apricot Jam
   * 1/2 bottle Russian Dressing
   * 1 pkg Onion Soup mix (optional)
* Instructions: Cook chicken breast for 30 mins (or 20 mins in boiling water). Combine jam and dressing (and onion soup mix if desired). Pour over chicken. Cook in oven for 25 mins.
Ham & Cheese Strata
* Ingredients:
   * 12 white bread slices
   * 1 1/2 cup (6oz) shredded mild cheddar cheese
   * 1 (10oz) pkg frozen broccoli (thawed & well drained)
   * 1 cup Ham
   * 1 (8oz) pkg cream cheese
   * 3 eggs
   * 1 cup milk
   * 1/2 tsp dry mustard
* Instructions: Place 6 bread slices on bottom of 12x8 dish. Cover with 1 cup cheddar cheese, broccoli, ham, and remaining bread slices cut in half diagonally. Beat cream cheese until light, add eggs one at a time mixing well. Blend in milk and mustard. Pour over bread. Top with remaining cheddar cheese. Bake at 350°F for 45 to 50 min. Let stand 10 min before serving.
Tuna Casserole
* Ingredients:
   * 1 (14oz) can tomatoes
   * 1 can cream of mushroom soup
   * 2 (6 1/2 oz) tins tuna
   * 3 cheese slices
   * Potato chips (crushed)
   * Pepper & salt
* Instructions: Mix tomatoes & mushroom soup well in a bowl. Line the bottom of a 2-quart casserole with potato chips. Add a layer of tuna. Add a layer of tomato mixture. Add another layer of tuna. Place cheese slices on top. Pour remaining tomato mixture over this. Bake for 1 hour at 350°F.

Cabbage Rolls
* Ingredients:
   * Medium cabbage

   * Onions (chopped)
   * 1 egg (beaten)

Lemon Delight Squares
* Crust: 2 cups Flour, 1 cup butter (soft), 1/2 cup chopped pecans, 2 tbsp white sugar. Put in ungreased 9x13 pan. Bake in preheated 375°F oven for 15 min. Cool completely.
* Filling: 2 (8oz) pkgs cream cheese (soft), 2 cups icing sugar. Mix with mixer and spread over cooled baked bottom. Refrigerate for 15 min.
* Topping: 2 Lemon Pie Fillings (cooked as directed). Spread on filling layer. Sprinkle with graham wafer crusts or powdered icing sugar. Refrigerate until set.
Date Squares
* Filling: Cook until soft: 1 package Dates, 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup hot water. Set aside to cool.
* Crust: Sift 1 1/2 c. flour, 1/2 tsp soda and 1/2 tsp salt. Add 1 1/2 cups rolled oats with 1 c. brown sugar. Cut in 1/2 c. butter. Mix well until it resembles coarse crumbs.
* Instructions: Place half of mixture in greased 8x12 pan. Cover with date filling and top with remainder of mixture, pressing gently and evenly. Bake for 25 minutes in 375°F oven or until delicately browned. Cut while hot.
Swirl of Choc Cheese Squares
* Crust: 1 cup graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup butter (melted), 3 tbsp sugar. Combine and press into 8-inch pan.
* Filling: 1 pkg (8oz) cream cheese, 3/4 cup undiluted evaporated milk, 1 egg, 2 tbsp flour, 2 tsp vanilla, 1/2 cup semi-sweet choc chips.
* Instructions: Beat cheese & 1/2 cup sugar. Beat in milk, egg, flour, vanilla until smooth. Melt chips. Stir 1/2 cup of cheese mixture into chocolate. Pour remaining cheese mixture over crust. Pour choc mixture over cheese mixture. Swirl together. Bake at 300°F for 40-45 minutes.
Mandarin Cake (Scratch)

* Topping: When cooked mix 3/4 cup Brown Sugar, 2 tsp butter melted and 3 tsp canned milk. Spread over top and put in oven for 10 min.
Orange Mandarine Cake (Cake Mix)
* Ingredients: 1 White Cake mix, Orange Jello, Dream Whip (2 envelopes), Can Mandarine Oranges.
* Instructions: Break up cake in a bowl. Mix Orange Jello using the juice off the oranges in place of cold water. Let set until starting to jell. Then mix prepared Dream Whip into Jello, fold, and then mix in can Mandarine Oranges. Fold mixture in with Crumbled Cake. Put in Tube Pan and put in fridge to set (2 hours). Then set out on plate and icing with Dream Whip and Decorate with Oranges.
Carrot Cake
* Ingredients:
   * 4 medium sized carrots (grated, should be 2 cups)
   * 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
   * 1 (8oz) can crushed pineapple (drained)
   * 2 cups sugar
   * 4 eggs
   * 1 1/2 cups Mazola oil
   * 2 cups Flour
   * 1 1/2 tsp soda
   * 2 tsp baking powder
   * 2 tsp cinnamon
   * 1 tsp salt
* Instructions: Set aside carrots, nuts, pineapple. Beat sugar and eggs together. Beat in oil. Stir into egg/oil mix: flour, soda, baking powder, cinnamon, salt. Then mix in carrots, pineapple & nuts. Bake 350°F 45-50 min.
* Icing: Blend 6 oz cream cheese, 6 tbsp butter, orange peel, 1 tsp vanilla, 2 cups icing sugar.

* Instructions: Cream shortening, add molasses & spices. Add eggs, beat well. Add flour, baking powder & salt alternating with hot water and soda. Bake in 325°F for 50-60 minutes.

Christmas Fruit Cake (Light/Creamed)
* Fruit: 6 cups raisins, 6 cups currants, 4 cups chopped dates, 2 cups glazed cherries, 1 cup chopped pineapple, 1 cup mixed fruit, 1 1/2 cups blanched almonds.
* Batter: Cream 3 cups Shortening. Blend in 5 cups brown sugar. Add 2 tsp each vanilla, lemon & almond extract. Beat until light and fluffy. Add 1 cup crushed pineapple and lemon rind. Beat 10 egg yolks until thick, add to sugar mixture. Blend or sift together 8 cups flour, 1 tsp Baking soda, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 1/2 tsp nutmeg, 3/4 tsp cloves. Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture alternately with 1 cup sour milk. Beat 10 egg whites until stiff. Fold whites into batter. Add prepared fruit & nuts. Bake at 325°F for 2 to 3 hours.

Mom's Steam Christmas Pudding

   * 2 cups cooked, chopped chicken (boneless, skinless works best)
   * 1 cup onion
   * 2 tbsp oil

Pork Chops Creole
Ingredients:


* 6 pork chops
* 1 cup diced celery
* 1 med onion (1/2 cup)
* 2 tsp chili powder
* 1 can red Kidney beans
* 1 can Kernel corn (12 oz)
* 1 cup regular rice
* 1 can tomato soup
* 1 soup can water
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp dry oregano (crumbled)
* 1/4 tsp pepper


Instructions:


1. Brown pork chops, remove and set aside. Drain all but 2 tbsp dripping from pan.
2. Saute celery and onion stir in chili powder cook 1 min.
3. Stir in kidney beans and liquid, corn and liquid, rice, tomato soup, water, salt, oregano and pepper mix well.
4. Bring mixture to boiling. Pour into a greased 12-cup shallow casserole, arrange browned chops on top. Cover.
5. Bake in mod oven 350 1 hr or until rice and chops are tender.

Quick French Omelette
1 - 1 1/2 min. to cook Ingredients:


* 6 grade A Large Eggs
* 2 tbsp water or tom juice
* Dash of pepper
* Seasoned Salt to Taste
* (Fillings: pinch of desired seasoning, crumbled rosemary, chopped chives or parsley paprika)


Instructions:


1. Use 10" pan. If using 12" pan add 2 to 4 eggs plus a tsp water per egg.
2. Heat pan to 380° to 400° (drop of water should dance).
3. Break eggs into bowl, add water, salt and pepper. Blend with fork, do not beat frothy.
4. Add enough salad oil to pan to coat bottom & sides.
5. Add egg mixture all at once cook until egg is completely set and still creamy. Spread evenly in pan.
6. Hold on serving plates in warm oven until all are completed, then serve.
